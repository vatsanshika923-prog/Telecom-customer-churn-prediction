## Overview

Telecom Customer Churn Prediction is a machine learning application that predicts whether a customer is likely to leave a telecom service provider based on customer demographics, subscription details, and service usage patterns.

The project helps telecom companies identify at-risk customers and take proactive retention measures, reducing customer attrition and improving business performance.

---

## Problem Statement

Customer churn is a major challenge for telecom companies. Acquiring new customers is often more expensive than retaining existing ones.

This project aims to:

* Identify customers likely to churn
* Analyze customer behavior patterns
* Support customer retention strategies
* Enable data-driven business decisions
